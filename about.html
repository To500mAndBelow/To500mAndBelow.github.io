Our CanSat

We recognize and clearly identify objects of interest

This allows you to perform different tasks that were not possible before or partake in them with increased efficiency. Our "pocket satellite", as a project of the Austrian CanSat team "T5B", has a wide range of capabilities in fields ranging from location of pipeline leaks and damaged power lines, to scientific research and military use.

We collect other data, such as temperature, air-pressure, etc.

This allows you to calculate many different things, such as the distance of the CanSat from the identified object, how accurate the tracking/maping is (which is affected by humidity, temperature and other factors at a given altitude), the GPS-coordinates and much more!

Innovative descend control design

The descend of the satellite is controlable and, therefore, allows to collect data from different areas. Furthermore, it increases the area of surveilance per 1 launch of the CanSat.

The "pocket-satellite" is the size of a can!

Therefore, the CanSat has a number of practical implications, where a local, immediate collection of the data, provided by our CanSat is required.
